Our story began in autumn 2016 in a refugee camp in Northern Greece. As volunteers for the NGO "A Drop in the Ocean" (DiH) we were responsible for the distribution of donations like clothes, hygiene products or food.

Back then DiH started their first "Free Shop" - a place where refugees could buy what they want and need for the first time.

It was based on a virtual currency called Drops which was given out to all resident in recurring intervals. Our job was to develop an application keeping track of the accounts and transactions. This is the start of Boxwise (or Drop App at that time).
